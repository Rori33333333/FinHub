Finance lingo 101: Terms


Basic terms

Swipe or scroll to level up your financial lingo one word at a time.

Income

Money that comes into your pocket or bank account.
'Money In'


Examples:
  • Salary
  • Grants
  • Investment payout

Expense

Money that you spend to buy or pay for things you want.
'Money Out'

Examples:

  • Subsciption
  • Utility Bills
  • Groceries

Needs / essentials

Things you must pay for to live safely and comfortably.

Examples:

  • Food
  • Water
  • Clothing

Wants / desires

Nice-to-have purchases that are optional rather than necessary.

Examples:


  • Video games
  • Snacks
  • Take away

Opportunity cost

The value of the next best choice you give up.
