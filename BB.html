What is a budget?

A budget is a plan on how to use your money. In a budget you allocate your monthly or weekly (depending on the time frame) income 'money in' to your various expenses 'money out'.

Why is a budget important?

Have you ever felt like your money just vanished into thin air or struggled to keep up with savings? The truth is your money is not a magician, you spent it. That is what a budget is for; to help you allocate your income to the things you need the most in order to prevent Houdini like tricks.
